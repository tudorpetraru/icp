Logic & usage

This diagram compares AI 2027, Situational Awareness, and AI 2040 as competing pathways through the same capability buildup. The shared spine is a forecast. The three branches are strategic choices with different control assumptions.

Source lenses

  • AI 2027 follows a private-lab race that reaches a late government branch between continued acceleration and slowdown.
  • Situational Awareness moves frontier development into a secure national project led by a democratic coalition.
  • AI 2040 uses compute declaration, verification, research transparency, broad diffusion, and a pause at top-human capability.

Read the structure

  • Left to right is scenario time. Dates are claims made by the source scenarios, not independent forecasts.
  • Thicker edges are strong gates; thinner edges are partial enablers; dashed edges are cross-couplings.
  • Pulsing nodes are maintained states: they decay without budgets, enforcement or public legitimacy.
  • Double-ring nodes are branch points, regime outcomes, or irreducible sinks.
  • Risk mode shows where each route can fail: theft, secrecy, covert compute, premature trust, or permanent emergency power.
