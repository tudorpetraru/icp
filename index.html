Logic & usage

A dependency graph with a schedule attached: if aligned ASI exists, what has to light first, what follows from it, and what stays hard regardless? Force layout with calmed edge weights, stronger fills and pill labels so nothing blends into the tangle.

Read the structure

  • Left to right is earliest maturity, not importance.
  • Thicker edges = strong enablers; thin = partial; dashed = feedback / meaning.
  • Pulsing nodes are maintained states that decay without ongoing work.
  • Double-ring nodes are irreducible sinks: governance, meaning, great-power peace.
  • Click a node to trace upstream (amber) and downstream (cyan).
