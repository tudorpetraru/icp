Logic & usage

This diagram compresses four AGI-transition PDFs into one policy dependency map. It treats the next phase as rails to install before triggers fire, not as a single forecast.

Read the structure

  • Left to right is the earliest useful installation point for a policy rail or monitoring gate.
  • Thicker edges are strong gates; thinner edges are partial enablers; dashed edges are cross-couplings.
  • Pulsing nodes are maintained states: they decay without budgets, enforcement or public legitimacy.
  • Double-ring nodes are regime outcomes or irreducible sinks.
  • Risk mode shows capture traps: oligopoly, proliferation, surveillance, rent capture and emergency overreach.
