Logic & usage

This diagram translates the 2026 multiplanetary-society assessment into a closure map. It is not a destination poster. It asks which gates must close before an outpost becomes a settlement, an industrial base, or a polity.

Read the structure

  • Left to right is base-case maturity or institutional readiness.
  • Thicker edges are strong gates; thinner edges are partial enablers; dashed edges are cross-couplings.
  • Pulsing nodes are maintained states: they decay without continuous budgets, skills or legitimacy.
  • Double-ring nodes are settlement outcomes whose weakest closure dimension can veto the tier.
  • Risk mode shows overclaim traps: transport theater, company-town governance and premature birth politics.
